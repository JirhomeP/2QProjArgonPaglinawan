Butterfly

The butterfly is a swimming stroke swum on the chest with both arms moving at the same time, accompanied by the butterfly, also called as dolphin, kick.

For many swimmers, butterfly is the hardest stroke to perform, as it requires more muscles firing at any one time.

In terms of speed, it's peak speed is higher than that of the other strokes.

Even if it's peak speed is high, the recovery speed is slow, which makes it overall speed still lower than the front crawl.
