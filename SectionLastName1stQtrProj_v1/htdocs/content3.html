Freestyle

Freestyle, commonly know as the front crawl, is regarded as the fastest out of all strokes used in competitive swimming.

This style is swum like an upside down backstroke, the alternating arm stroke makes for a relatively constant speed throughout the cycle.

The arms alternate from side to side, so while one arm is pulling and pushing under the water, the other arm is recovering above the water.
