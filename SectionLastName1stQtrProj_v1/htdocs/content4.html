Breaststroke

It is sometimes referred to as the "frog" stroke, as the arms and legs move somewhat like a frog swimming in the water.

The swimmer's head is out of the water a large portion of the time, and so it can be swum easily at slow speeds.

In terms of speed, It is the slowest and most comfortable stroke among the others.
